Crowd Motivation

Updated 5/3/2019

[index]

Some of the most intelligent people alive are dedicating their working careers to selling advertisements. How can we get people to do more important things?

Money is a strong motivator, but asymptotic in its effects on behavior. When the most urgent problems arise, will people solve them? Suppose some catastrophy occurs which requires n hours of human labor at m efficiency to subvert. How can we build an incentivization structure to subvert disaster?

One of the most common success conditions for startups is desperation. It seems unlikely, however, that the same can be said for large scale, collaborative changes.
