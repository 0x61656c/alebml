Once in a while, I look back at my past self and am shocked by how stupid I sounded back then. This phenomenon is, for the most part, medium-agnostic. I wonder whether it is a consequence of compounding returns on content, or one of getting older and projecting an image of my current self on to my past self.

Someone wiser than me says that as you grow older, the frequency of this process decreases, though probably as a sign of weakness and complacency more than wisdom.

Life is short, and there are immense problems that still need to be solved. Why stop growing? Today I turned twenty-one, and I feel much less stupid than the me of yesterday. Tomorrow I'll probably feel differently.

But now is the only time I can get shit done.
