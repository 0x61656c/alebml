Interviewing at YC

Updated 5/1/2019

[index]

Yesterday, Pairi interviewed at YC. We were grilled by a panel of really cool people, and we managed to hold our own. I think we feel a lot more comfortable pitching, because almost nothing could be higher pressure than what we just went through.

Overall, despite the fact that we didn't get in, we both felt that it was an incredibly motivating and positive experience, for Pairi as a company and us as individuals. Thanks to Gustaf and Holly in particular for your feedback!
